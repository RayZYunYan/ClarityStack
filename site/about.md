<section class="prose-block">
  <p class="eyebrow">About ClarityStack</p>
  <h1>AI news for builders, not spectators.</h1>

  <p>ClarityStack is a daily AI news system built for people who actually ship — not just follow the hype cycle. It surfaces meaningful developments across AI research, tooling, and infrastructure, filtered through a simple question: <em>does this matter if you're building something real?</em></p>

  <p>This is not a news aggregator. Every post includes opinionated analysis — what I would use, what I would ignore, and where I think the real leverage is. The goal is to replace noise with signal, and give you the kind of context you'd normally get from a sharp engineer on your team.</p>

  <h2>How It Works</h2>

  <p>ClarityStack runs on a fully automated pipeline that I designed and built from scratch — optimized for reliability, controllability, and zero-trust execution:</p>

  <ul>
    <li><strong>Source ingestion</strong> — ArXiv, GitHub Trending, and Hacker News are continuously scanned for high-signal AI developments.</li>
    <li><strong>Structured extraction</strong> — Gemini Flash converts raw content into structured JSON, extracting key facts, relevance signals, and source traceability.</li>
    <li><strong>Opinionated synthesis</strong> — Claude generates first-person analysis using a strict style guide that enforces clarity, specificity, and non-generic opinions.</li>
    <li><strong>Privacy enforcement</strong> — A dual-pass privacy scanner runs before and after generation to guarantee no sensitive data leaks into output.</li>
    <li><strong>Human gating</strong> — Every post requires explicit approval. A custom Discord bot pushes drafts to my phone for review before anything is published.</li>
    <li><strong>Multi-platform delivery</strong> — Approved content is automatically deployed to this blog (Cloudflare Pages) and LinkedIn. Automated publishing to X is planned.</li>
  </ul>

  <p>The system runs inside an NVIDIA NemoClaw sandbox with kernel-level isolation (Landlock + seccomp), strict network whitelisting, and effectively zero marginal cost.</p>

  <p>This is not just a content pipeline — it's a controlled AI system: deterministic where it matters, automated where it scales.</p>

  <h2>About Me</h2>

  <p>I'm <strong>Rui Zhang</strong> (Ray), a software engineer focused on AI systems, security architecture, and developer tooling.</p>

  <p>I built ClarityStack as both a daily-use product and a proof of how AI automation should be done: multi-model orchestration, strong safety boundaries, and human-in-the-loop control that doesn't become a bottleneck.</p>

  <p>My current interests sit at the intersection of AI agents, system safety, and real-world deployment — building systems that are not just powerful, but predictable and trustworthy.</p>

  <p>You can find me on <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/ray-zhang-cs-cn">LinkedIn</a> or explore the <a href="https://github.com/RayZYunYan/ClarityStack">ClarityStack source code on GitHub</a>.</p>
