  <p class="eyebrow">About ClarityStack</p>
  <h1>Human-approved AI publishing, with privacy guardrails built in.</h1>
  <p>ClarityStack is a dark-tech publication workflow for AI news, papers, and engineering analysis. The pipeline fetches source material, scans it for sensitive patterns, drafts platform-specific content, and keeps a human approval gate in the loop before anything ships.</p>
  <p>The stack is designed to be useful for builders: practical summaries, original links preserved, and a publishing flow that can run locally or inside a future OpenClaw / NemoClaw deployment.</p>
